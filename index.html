Adebisi Oluwatosin Ayodele

Architect and 3d visualizer, Founder of Vues projects limited. and Interiora.ng.

Adebisi oluwatosin was born on the 16th day of june 1986 in a sleepy town of Ado ekiti, Ekiti state, Nigeria to the family of Mr Taiwo and Mrs Folake Adebisi. He attended primary and secondary school in Lagos and then proceeded to the federal university of technology, Akure to study architecture between 2005 and 2011.
